How it works

Organize your life in minutes, not days.

LekhaJokha is designed to feel natural. No complicated setup. Just start and build your system gradually.

1

Add your documents

Tap the purple + button on your mobile screen to upload documents, photos, videos, or bills. Create folders the way you think—“Car”, “Home”, “Doctor's Presciptions”, “Kids School”.

2

Track tasks & reminders

Add your ToDo items and expiry reminders. When a task is done, tap once. Unfinished items roll over automatically to the next day.

3

Secure everything

Lock your Personal Vault with a PIN. Keep sensitive documents private. Everything stays on your device.

4

Backup & stay in control

Backup locally and restore anytime. Track your expenses with a 12-month graph and see spending in one currency.

  • No judgment, just order: Name folders the way you think: “First Job”, “Car – Dad”, “Kids – School”, “Sales – Europe Trip”.
